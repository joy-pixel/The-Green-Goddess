About Us

We are a small group of passionate hobbyist gardeners who wanted to share our love for gardening and flowers to the rest of the world.


For more than a decade; we've managed to grow an extraordinary amount of trees and flowers all over the country with our student 4k clubs.

Research is at the heart of our operation, and we conduct extensive trials to find the very best cut flower varieties,seedlings,planting medium and the latest tech in agriculture and forestry to aid in our work

What We Do

We are an NGO that acts as a middleman between overseas donors and 4k clubs in schools. It is through this funding that we are able to offer boyh financial and material aid to all our member schools


Our goal is to teach peaceful cohabitation with the earth to the younger generations,ensuring they learn from early on the need to protect and cherish the environment for the benefit of everyone .

Sustainability of our projects matters to us ,this way,it ensures the work we do today will still hold it's weight years to come
